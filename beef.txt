Ingredients:

Dough:
250 g flour
150 mL water, lukewarm
7 g dry yeast
6 g salt, fine
15 g olive oil, extra virgin
1 teaspoon sugar

Toppings:
1 steak (thinly sliced, seared - see Instructions)
350 g tomato frito
100 g fresh mozzarella, grated
30 g pecorino or parmesan, grated
1 tablespoon pine nuts
fresh arugula
olive oil
pepper and salt, freshly ground

Instructions: 

Dough:
Mix the sugar and yeast into the lukewarm water in a large bowl and leave for a few minutes until it foams
Sift the flour over the bowl, add olive oil and knead it into a smooth dough (preferrably with KitchenAid armed with dough hooks)
Add salt during kneading
Make a ball from the dough, grease the bowl with olive oil and cover the ball with the oil, leaving the ball in
Cover with a damp tea towel and let the dough rise for an hour or until its volume has doubled
Use flour to roll the dough into 2 smaller balls and let rise for another 30-45 minutes

Pizza:
Pre-heat the oven to 250°C (insert a pizza stone if available)
Use flour to roll the balls into pizza bases
Spread the tomato sauce and mozzarella evenly
Heat for 8-12 minutes
Meanwhile:
Roast the pine nuts in an empty pan until golden brown
Season the beef with pepper and salt and sear for 1-2 minutes in butter, then let it rest in aluminium foil for 5 minutes
Thinly slice the beef
When done, remove the pizza(s) from the oven and top with the beef, arugula and roasted pine nuts
Add grated pecorino/parmesan, drizzle some olive oil on top and optionally ground some more pepper to taste before serving
