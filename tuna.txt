Ingredients:

1 or 2 canned tuna (on water), divided
6 tablespoons tomato frito
2 teaspoons oregano
40 g cheese, grated
40 g anchovies (drained, 80g incl. oil)
1 tablespoon capers
1 red onion, chopped
a dash of olive oil

Pizzadough:

250 g flour
7 g dry yeast
150 mL lukewarm water
1 teaspoon sugar
1 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil
1 big pinch of salt

Instructions:

First make the dough
Dissolve the yeast and sugar in the lukewarm water
Sift the flour over a bowl, make a well and add salt into it
Pour the water mixture in and add the olive oil
Gently stir with a fork before mixing it with a KitchenAid equipped with dough hooks - alternatively, knead by hand - for 15 minutes
Ball the dough, put it in a greased bowl and cover with a damp tea towel, let the dough rise for 2 hours

Pre-heat the oven to 220°C
Divide the dough and use a rolling pin to make 2 round pizzas
Spread the tomato frito over the base and sprinkle them with the oregano
Evenly spread the tuna, anchovies, red onion, capers and cheese over the pizza bases
Brush the edges of the pizza with a little olive oil and heat for 10-12 minutes
